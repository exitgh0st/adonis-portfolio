Project Overview

StayHub is a comprehensive property and tenancy management system designed to streamline rental property operations for landlords and tenants. The platform automates complex billing calculations, manages tenant relationships, tracks utility consumption through submeter readings, and provides real-time access to financial information for all stakeholders.

Problem Statement

Property management, particularly in multi-unit residential buildings, presents several critical challenges:

Manual Bill Calculation

Landlords spend significant time manually calculating utility bills based on submeter readings. Each unit operates on a submeter connected to the main utility meter (MERALCO for electricity, Maynilad for water), requiring proportional cost allocation based on consumption.

Complex Utility Distribution

When multiple units share a single utility statement (main meter), calculating each unit's share based on their submeter readings involves complex proportional calculations that are error-prone when done manually.

Limited Transparency

Tenants lack real-time visibility into their billing status, pending payments, and payment history, leading to confusion and disputes.

Inefficient Communication

Announcing maintenance schedules, policy updates, or important notices requires individual contact with each tenant.

Document Management

Storing and accessing tenancy contracts, utility bills, and payment records involves cumbersome paper-based systems or disorganized digital files.

Solution Overview

StayHub addresses these challenges through a comprehensive web-based platform that automates billing calculations, centralizes property management operations, and provides role-based portals for both landlords and tenants.

Key Technical Challenges & Solutions

1. Automated Utility Bill Generation

Challenge: Automating the calculation of individual unit bills based on submeter readings and main utility statements. Each property receives a single utility bill (e.g., MERALCO electricity bill), but individual units operate on submeters requiring proportional cost allocation.

Solution: Implemented a multi-step calculation algorithm that retrieves the utility statement, fetches associated unit readings, calculates cost per unit (₱/kWh = Total Cost ÷ Total Consumption), and multiplies each unit's consumption by this rate.

Example: For a ₱5,500 bill with 21 kWh total, Unit A1 consuming 9 kWh pays (9 ÷ 21) × ₱5,500 = ₱2,357.14.

2. Data Integrity & Complex Relationships

Challenge: Managing complex relationships between utility statements, units, readings, and bills while ensuring data consistency.

Solution: Created junction tables (UtilityStatementRecord) linking statements to units, used Prisma transactions for atomic bill generation, implemented cascade delete with protection for records with existing bills, and added validation middleware.

3. Role-Based Access Control

Challenge: Strict separation between admin and tenant capabilities while maintaining security.

Solution: JWT-based authentication with role claims, backend middleware for token validation and role checking, frontend route guards (AuthGuard), service-layer data filtering ensuring tenants only see their own information, and bcrypt password hashing.

4. Professional PDF Generation

Challenge: Professional, printable bill statements with branding, QR codes, and meter verification photos.

Solution: Integrated jsPDF with custom templates generating two-page documents including property branding, detailed consumption breakdown, QR codes for payment, bank transfer instructions, actual meter reading photos, billing period dates, and terms & conditions.

Results & Impact

Operational Efficiency

  • Eliminated Manual Calculations: Automated utility bill generation completely removed manual calculation requirements, saving significant time each billing cycle.
  • Reduced Billing Errors: Automated proportional cost distribution ensures mathematical accuracy, eliminating human calculation errors and potential disputes.
  • Streamlined Property Management: Centralized platform replaced multiple disconnected systems and paper-based processes.

Tenant Experience

  • Real-Time Bill Visibility: Instant access to billing information without contacting landlord or waiting for paper statements.
  • Billing Transparency: Detailed breakdown with actual meter photos provides complete transparency, reducing disputes and building trust.
  • Professional Documentation: Professional PDF statements with QR codes and payment instructions simplify payment processing.

Conclusion

StayHub demonstrates how modern web technologies can solve real-world property management challenges. By automating complex utility bill calculations and providing transparent, accessible platforms for both landlords and tenants, the system significantly improves operational efficiency while enhancing the tenant experience. The successful implementation of automated proportional billing based on submeter readings represents a significant technical achievement that delivers tangible value to property managers and tenants alike.
